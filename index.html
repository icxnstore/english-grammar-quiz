Question 1

Identify the tense used in the following sentence:

"She is reading a book right now."

What is the tense?

  • a) Present Simple
  • b) Present Continuous
  • c) Past Perfect
  • d) Future Simple

Question 2

Fill in the blank with the correct form of the verb:

"He ______ (play) football every Sunday."

Select the correct verb form:

  • a) playing
  • b) plays
  • c) played
  • d) will play

Answer Key

1. Present Continuous - "She is reading" indicates an ongoing action happening right now.

2. Plays - "He plays football" describes a habitual action.
